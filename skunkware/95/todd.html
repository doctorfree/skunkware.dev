Todd Rundgren

Todd Rundgren is launching an online artist subscription service. The service basically allows consumers/fans to underwrite a creator's work directly, to receive the content as it is produced, and in the process, to minimize the hassles involved with the forecasting, inventory and delivery of media products. Beginning in May, Todd will offer consumers a year's worth of his creative output by online subscription over the Internet.
