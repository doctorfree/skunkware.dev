Software Archive Sites

The following sites offer archives of software for SCO Unix and SCO Xenix. In many cases programs are available in binary and/or custom diskette form for those without the SCO software development products. These archive sites have no official affiliation with SCO. We are grateful for the service they may provide to you. Remember, the great majority of Xenix software will also perform satisfactorily in Unix and Opendesktop environments. The archive sites are:
  • HTML version of SCO FTP sites FAQ
  • SCO Software Download Center
  • SCO Internet Resources
  • SCO Skunkware
  • UnixWare Freebird Web Site
  • UnixWare Freebird ftp archives
  • Celestial Systems, Inc.
  • Wimsey
  • XeniTec Consulting Services
  • Oakland University (Xenix)
  • SIU (Unix and Opendesktop)
  • X11 Clients
  • X11 contributed source at sunsite.unc.edu
  • Robert Lipe's Beta GNU Dev Sys (Q4 95)
The most recent version of the SCO FTP sites FAQ may be obtained by anonymous ftp to ftp.celestial.com as the file README.sco-sites.
