To determine the licensing conditions for a particular component, see the corresponding source archive at either OpenServer 5 and UnixWare 7 Skunkware source or OpenServer 6 Skunkware source.

As a convenience, a list of components and their licenses has been prepared based on the information in the RPM spec file for that component.
