Installing Linux Applications

  • Linux a.out and statically linked ELF binaries may be installed in /usr/local/linux/bin and symbolic links by the name of the binary created from /usr/local/bin to lxfront

  • Dynamically linked Linux ELF binaries may be installed anywhere in the execution path

  • RPM format distributions can be installed using native or lxrun rpm with arguments
