Join the Mailing Lists

The Caldera Skunkware announcements mailing list is a moderated list for announcements of new and updated Open Source packages precompiled and packaged for Caldera platforms. To join the Caldera Skunkware announcements mailing list, send an e-mail message to skunkware-announce-request@johndoe.pdev.ca.caldera.com with a message body of the one word "subscribe" (no quotes).

The Polecats mailing list is an unmoderated list for discussion between developers writing, porting, packaging and maintaining Open Source projects for Caldera platforms. To join the Polecats mailing list, send an e-mail message to polecats-request@johndoe.pdev.ca.caldera.com with a message body of the one word "subscribe" (no quotes).

Be sure to send the e-mail subscription from the account to which you wish to have the list postings sent.
